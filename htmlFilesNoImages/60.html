God is infinitely gracious.
He waits for us to return to Him by heart-humiliation,
confession, and repentance.
He will have mercy on all,
and will save all who cherish contrition of soul.
The renunciation of self-confidence prepares the way
for true faith in God.
The moment human beings renounce their
selfishness, covetousness, and idolatry,
that moment God becomes their all-sufficient Helper.
In the infinite fulness of His grace He imparts,
for time and for eternity,
whatever is needed for the souls and bodies
of those who believe.
