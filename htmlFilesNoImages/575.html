Motion song if desired.
1. — Hands lifted from left knee to right shoulder.
2. — Arms folded and rocked.
3. — Hands moved downward from head to knees.
4. — Hands lifted.
5. — Hands move upward.
6. — Hands move downward from left to right, ﬁngers working.
7. — Flowing motion.
8. — Hands move downward.
9. — Point to sun.
10. — Hands indication “Milky Way.”
11, 12, 13. — Point to location of each in the sky.
14. — Hands move downward.
15. — Pointing over the sea.
16. — Arms extended, hands open, showing offerings.
