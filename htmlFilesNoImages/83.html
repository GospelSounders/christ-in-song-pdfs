MERCY’S FREE (2)MERCY’S FREE (2)
“ ‘Not by works of righteousness which we have done,
but according to his mercy he saved us’ (Titus 3:5).
When Satan tells you that you are a sinner,
and cannot hope to receive blessing from God,
tell him that Christ came into the world to save sinners.
We have nothing to recommend us to God;
but the plea that we may urge now and ever
is our utterly helpless condition that makes
His redeeming power a necessity.
Renouncing all self-dependence,
we may look to the cross of Calvary and say,-—
‘In my hand no price I bring;
Simply to Thy cross I cling.’ ”
