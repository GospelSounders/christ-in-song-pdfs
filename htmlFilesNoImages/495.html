TRUST AND OBEY (2)
Copyright, 1887, by D. B. Towner
“We shall be brought into strait places;
but we do not want to wait until then before we learn to trust and obey.
Now, just now, is our day of opportunity and privilege.
When the light of truth is shining upon us, we are to learn the lesson.
Let us plead with God to give us a true conception of His character
and a willingness to obey Him.”
The General Conference Bulletin 3-30-1903
