What shall I say to arouse the remnant people of God?...
I warn all who profess the name of Christ
to closely examine themselves
and make full and thorough confession of all their wrongs,
that they may go beforehand to judgment,
and that the recording angel
may write pardon opposite their names.
My brother, my sister,
if these precious moments of mercy are not improved,
you will be left without excuse.
If you make no special effort to arouse,
if you will not manifest zeal in repenting,
these golden moments will soon pass,
and you will be weighed in the balance and found wanting.
