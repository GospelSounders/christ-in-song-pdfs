The most precious fruit of sanctification is the grace of meekness.
When this grace presides in the soul
the disposition is molded by its influence.
There is a continual waiting upon God and a submission of the will to His.
The understanding grasps every divine truth
and the will bows to every divine precept,
without doubting or murmuring.
