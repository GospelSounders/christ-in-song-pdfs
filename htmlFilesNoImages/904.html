DAUGHTER OF ZION (2)DAUGHTER OF ZION (2)
We are homeward bound.
He who loved us so much as to die for us hath builded for us a city.
The New Jerusalem is our place of rest.
There will be no sadness in the city of God.
No wail of sorrow, no dirge of crushed hopes
and buried affections, will evermore be heard.
Soon the garments of heaviness will be changed for the wedding garment.
Soon we shall witness the coronation of our King.
Those whose lives have been hidden with Christ,
those who on this earth have fought the good fight of faith,
will shine forth with the Redeemers glory in the kingdom of God.
Maranatha 352
