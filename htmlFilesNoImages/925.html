Jesus said, This gospel of the kingdom
shall be preached in all the world
for a witness unto all nations (Matthew 24:14).
His kingdom will not come until the good tidings of His grace
have been carried to all the earth.
Hence, as we give ourselves to God,
and win other souls to Him,
we hasten the coming of His Kingdom.
Only those who devote themselves to His service...
pray in sincerity, Thy kingdom come....
The petition, Thy will be done in earth, as it is in heaven,
is a prayer that the reign of evil on this earth may be ended,
that sin may be forever destroyed,
and the kingdom of righteousness be established.
Then in earth as in heaven will be fullled
all the good pleasure of his goodness
(II Thessalonians 1:11).
