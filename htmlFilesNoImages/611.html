We see not the hand that is guiding us; we cannot, like Israel of old,
look upon the pillar of cloud and the pillar of fire,
and we hear not the voice that spoke to them from the mount.

that the great Shepherd of Israel has been leading us all the way in the past,
and that the path where He leads will be safe for all who follow Him,
even in the stormy days of trial and conflict.
