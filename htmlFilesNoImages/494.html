THE CROSS THAT HE GAVE (2)THE CROSS THAT HE GAVE (2)
By permission
Will you follow the promptings of an unsanctified heart,
turn away from self-denial for Christs sake,
and step over the cross without lifting it?
Or will you lift that cross, heavy though it may be,
and make some sacrifice for the truths sake?
May God help you to see where you are,
that you may place a true estimate upon eternal things.
