THE PASSOVER (2)
Copyright, 1899, by F. E. Belden
Jesus “says, ‘Behold, I stand at the door, and knock:
if any man hear my voice, and open the door, I will come in to him,
and will sup with him, and he with me’ (Revelation 3:20).
How willing is Christ to take possession of the soul temple
if we will let Him!
He is represented as waiting
and knocking at the door of the heart.
Then why does He not enter?
It is because the love of sin has closed the door of the heart.
As soon as we consent to give sin up,
to acknowledge our guilt,
the barrier is removed between the soul and the Saviour.”
