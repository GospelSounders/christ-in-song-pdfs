If we are overcome let us not delay to repent,
and to accept the pardon that will place us on vantage ground.
If we repent and believe,
the cleansing power from God will be ours.
His saving grace is freely offered.
His pardon is given to all who will receive it....
Over every sinner that repents
the angels of God rejoice with songs of joy.
Not one sinner need be lost.
Full and free is the gift of saving grace.
Gods Amazing Grace 179
The shepherds search ceases not
until the lost is brought back to the fold.
The woman whose coin is lost searches till she nds it.
These parables do not speak of failure
but of success and joy in the recovery of the lost.
Here is the divine guarantee that not one lost soul is overlooked,
not one is left unsuccored.
With all our efforts in seeking for the lost,
Christ will cooperate.
