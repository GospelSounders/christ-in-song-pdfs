THE THOUGHT OF GOD
(St. Johns. C. M.)
F. L. Hosmer James Turle
434
Who has the heart? With whom are our thoughts?
Of whom do we love to converse?
Who has our warmest affections and our best energies?
If we are Christs, our thoughts are with Him,
and our sweetest thoughts are of Him.
All we have and are is consecrated to Him.
We long to bear His image, breathe His spirit, do His will,
and please Him in all things.
