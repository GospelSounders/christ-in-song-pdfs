Your warfare is accomplished. Enter into the joy of thy Lord.
Blessed, blessed benediction;
I want to hear it from His immortal lips.
I want to praise Him; I want to honor Him that sitteth on the throne.
I want my voice to echo and re-echo through the courts of heaven.
Will you be there?...
God help us, and fill us with all fullness and power,
and then we can taste of the joys of the world to come."
