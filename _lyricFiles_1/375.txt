.For the  beauty of the earth, For the glory of theat skies, For the  love which from our birth Over and around usis lies, Lord of all, to Thee we  raise This our grateful song of praise.  2.For the joy of human love, Brother, sister, parent, child, Friends on  earth and Friend above, Pleasures pure and undefiled, Lord of all, to Thee we  raise This our grateful song of praise.  3.For the  gift of Thy dear Son, For the hope of heav'n  last, For the  Spirit's vict'ry won, For the crown when life  past, Lord of all, to Thee we  raise Songs of gratitude and praise.
