. Saviour, lead me lest I stray,    Gently lead me all the way;    I am safe when by Thy side,    I would in Thy love abide  (love abide). 2. Thou the refuge of my soul,    When life's stormy billows roll;    I am safe when Thou art nigh,    All my hopes on Thee rely  (I rely). 3. Saviour, lead me till at last,    When the storm of life is past,    I shall reach the land of day,    Where all tears are wiped away  (wiped away). Lead me, lead me, Saviour, lead me, lest I stray;   Gently down the stream of time, Lead me, Saviour, all the way.
