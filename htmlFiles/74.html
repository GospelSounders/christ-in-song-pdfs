“The Bible opens to us the words of life;
for it makes us acquainted with Christ who is our life.
In order to have true, abiding faith in Christ,
we must know Him as He is represented in the word....
And faith must be kept in constant exercise through the diligent,
persevering study of the word.
The word thus becomes a living agency;
and we are sanctified through the truth.”
