“It would be well for us to spend a thoughtful hour each day
in contemplation of the life of Christ.
We should take it point by point, and let the imagination grasp each scene,
especially the closing ones.
As we thus dwell upon His great sacrifice for us,
our confidence in Him will be more constant,
our love will be quickened,
and we shall be more deeply imbued with His spirit.”
