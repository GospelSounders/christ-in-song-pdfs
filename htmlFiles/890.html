SWEEPING THROUGH THE GATES (2)SWEEPING THROUGH THE GATES (2)
The gates of the city will swing back on their glittering hinges,
and the nations that have kept the truth will enter in.
A crown will be placed on every head.
The words will be spoken, Come, ye blessed of my Father,
inherit the kingdom prepared for you from the foundation of the world.
Whom is it prepared for?For the obedient;
those who keep His commandments, and do His will....
As holy angels touch their harps, He wants you to follow,
singing the song of triumph in the city of God.
