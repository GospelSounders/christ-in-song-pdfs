BUILDING FOR ETERNITY (2)
Copyright, 1887, by D. B. Towner. Used by permission
“We should make daily advancement in the work of character-building.
When we try to separate from us our sinful habits,
it may at times seem that we are tearing ourselves all to pieces;
but this is the very work that we must do if we would grow up
unto the full stature of men and women in Christ Jesus,
if we would become fit temples for the indwelling of the Holy Spirit....
If we make an entire surrender to Him,
leave our life of sin and passion and pride,
and cling to Christ and His merits,
He will fulfill to us that He has promised.”
