Resting in the love of Christ,
trusting the Redeemer and Life-giver to work out for you
the salvation of your soul, you will know,
as you draw nearer and still nearer to Him,
what it means to endure the seeing of Him who is invisible.
God desires us to rest content in His love.
The contentment that Christ bestows is a gift worth infinitely more
than gold and silver and precious stones....
