HAPPY DAY (2)HAPPY DAY (2)
Deal truly with your own soul.
And then as you view yourself a sinner,
fall, all broken, at the foot of the cross.
Jesus will receive you, all polluted as you are,
and will wash you in His blood,
and cleanse you from all pollution,
and make you fit for the society of heavenly angels,
in a pure, harmonious heaven.
There is no jar, no discord, there.
All is health, happiness, and joy.
