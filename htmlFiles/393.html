THY MERCIES
(Geneva. C. M.)
2. Unnumbered comforts to my soul
Thy tender care bestowed,
Before my infant heart discerned
From whom those blessings owed.
4. O, how can words with equal warmth
The gratitude declare
That glows within my raptured heart?
But Thou canst read it there.
3. Ten thousand precious gifts
My daily thanks employ;
Nor is the least a cheerful heart,
That tastes those gifts with joy.
5. Through all eternity, to Thee
A joyful song Ill raise:
But O, eternitys too short
To utter all Thy praise!
