TELL IT TO JESUS (2)TELL IT TO JESUS (2)
By permission
The Spirit of God co-operates with the humble worker
that abides in Christ and communes with Him.
Pray when you are faint-hearted.
When you are desponding, close the lips firmly to men;
keep all the darkness within, lest you shadow the path of another,
but tell it to Jesus.
Ask for humility, wisdom, courage, increase of faith,
that you may see light in His light, and rejoice in His love.
Only believe, and you shall surely see the salvation of God.
The Review and Herald 4-22-1884
